Rose M. Schneider

Where do our number concepts come from?

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of California, San Diego working with David Barner. Broadly, I am interested in how children discover the logical properties of the symbolic number system. Some of my work explores how children learn about complex concepts, such as infinity and equality, from the building blocks of number, such as the count list and one-to-one correspondence.

I recently completed a master's degree at the University of Pennsylvania with Liz Brannon, and previously managed the Stanford Language and Cognition Lab under Michael Frank. My undergraduate degree is from Johns Hopkins University where I researched cognitive development and visual cognition. When not thinking about number and language, I like cooking, coding, yoga, taking pictures of my pup, and playing DnD.
